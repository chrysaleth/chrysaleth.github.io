Avarice

A hunger for the lost, an injustice I alone have wrought.

A creature of my own creation follows, an unyielding torment.

A palace I built, I alone the architect, yet I rot within its golden halls.

A cold sorrow wraps me, and in its chill my heart almost beats again.

A feeling, regret; an ache in the body, a plague on the mind, an indent upon the heart.
